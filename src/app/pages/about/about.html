WHO WE ARE

We are Team Mechaholics, a dedicated student-based team specializing in the design and manufacturing of cost-efficient quad bikes (ATVs). Over the past decade, our focus has been on optimizing the manufacturing process to deliver high-quality, performance-driven vehicles while maintaining strict cost control.

Our team's experience in cost-effective design and efficient production methods has been honed through continuous learning and a commitment to innovation. We strive to achieve excellence in every aspect of ATV manufacturing, with a keen emphasis on minimizing costs without compromising on quality or performance.
