Events We Participate In

H-BAJA 2026

hBAJA SAE India is a national-level engineering design competition organized by SAEINDIA, where student teams design and build a human-powered all-terrain vehicle to tackle real-world mobility challenges. The competition tests teams through static events such as design evaluation, cost analysis, and technical presentations, along with dynamic events that assess vehicle performance, durability, maneuverability, and safety on rough terrains. hBAJA provides students with hands-on experience in vehicle design, manufacturing, and teamwork while promoting innovation in sustainable mobility solutions.

IN-SPACe

Indian National Space Promotion and Authorization Center (IN-SPACe) is an autonomous agency under the Department of Space that promotes and regulates private participation in India’s space sector. It authorizes and supports non-government companies, startups, and institutions to undertake space activities. IN-SPACe also facilitates access to ISRO facilities, expertise, and infrastructure. The organization aims to encourage innovation and develop a strong commercial space ecosystem in India. It plays a key role in enabling collaboration between Indian Space Research Organisation and private space companies.

QUAD TORC

QuadTorc, organized by ISNEE Motorsports, is a national-level off-road competition that challenges student teams to design, analyze, and fabricate an ATV in compliance with technical standards, evaluates teams through rigorous static and dynamic rounds, including design validation, technical inspection, maneuverability, acceleration, and endurance tests, provides hands-on exposure to vehicle dynamics, fabrication, and project management, fostering innovation, teamwork, and industry-oriented engineering skills.

QBDC

The Quad Bike Design Challenge (QBDC), organized by FMAE, is a national-level competition where students design, fabricate, and test a single-rider all-terrain vehicle (ATV). It provides practical exposure to vehicle design, analysis, manufacturing, and performance evaluation while enhancing teamwork, technical skills, and project management experience in automotive engineering.
